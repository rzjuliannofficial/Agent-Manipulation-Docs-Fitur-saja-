# JIP (Jurnal Informatika Polinema) Formatting Guidelines

This document serves as the official reference format for JIP journal submissions.

## Page Layout & Margins
* **Paper Size**: A4 (210 mm x 297 mm)
* **Margins**:
  * Top: 30 mm
  * Left: 30 mm
  * Bottom: 20 mm
  * Right: 20 mm
* **Layout**: Two-column format for the main body (after the abstract).
* **Length**: Minimum of 6 pages.
* **Header/Footer**: Margin of 15 mm. Do not include page numbers, headers, or footers in the submission file.

## Typography
* **Font**: Times New Roman (TNR)
* **Main Title**: 14pt, Bold, Centered, max 10 words.
* **Authors**: 10pt, Bold, Centered.
* **Affiliations & Email**: 10pt, Centered.
* **Abstract & Keywords**: 10pt, Justified (Abstract) / Bold (Keywords). Single column layout. 200-250 words.
* **Body Text**: 10pt, Justified, 1.15 line spacing.
* **Headings**:
  * Heading 1 (e.g., **Pendahuluan**, **Metode**): 10pt, Bold.
  * Heading 2 (e.g., **2.1 Persamaan**): 10pt, Bold.

## Figures, Tables, & Equations
* **Equations**: Numbered sequentially in parentheses on the right side of the column: `(1)`.
* **Figures**: Label and title placed **below** the figure (e.g., `Gambar 1. Diagram alir sistem`). Font size 8pt, centered.
* **Tables**: Label and title placed **above** the table (e.g., `Tabel 1. Pengujian Hyperparameter`). Font size 8pt, centered.

## References
* **Style**: APA Style.
* **Source Ratio**: Minimum 80% primary library sources (journals).
* **Recency**: Up-to-date (last 5 years).
* **Minimum Quantity**: At least 15 references.
* **Reference Manager**: Mendeley or similar tool is required.
